Brief start...

  • 1971: Formed in 1971 by French doctors;
  • 1972: Earthquake in Managua, Nicaraguan capital, and first MSF mission;
  • 1974: Hurricane Fifi, Honduras, MSF first long-term medical relief mission;
  • 1975 - 1979: South Vietnam had fallen to North Vietnam, millions of Cambodians emigrated to Thailand, then MSF had their first refugee camp mission;
  • 1976 - 1984: MSF spent nine years assisting surgeries in the hospitals of various cities in Lebanon, during the Lebanese Civil War;
  • 1984: The situation in Lebanon deteriorated further and security for aid groups was minimised, MSF withdrew its volunteers.
  • 1989: MSF started long-term relief missions to help survivors of the mass killings and reconstruct Cambodia's health care system;
